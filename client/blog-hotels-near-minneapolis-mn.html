Hotels Near Minneapolis, MN: Your Complete Guide

June 1, 2024 5 min read

Minneapolis, Minnesota's largest city and a major cultural hub, offers a diverse range of accommodations from luxury hotels in Downtown to charming boutique properties in the North Loop. Whether you're visiting for business, sports, or exploring the city's attractions, finding the right place to stay in Minneapolis is essential for a great experience. This guide will help you navigate Minneapolis's hotel scene.

Best Areas to Stay in Minneapolis

Minneapolis's neighborhoods each offer unique experiences:

  • Downtown: Business district with luxury hotels
  • North Loop: Artsy area with boutique hotels
  • Uptown: Entertainment district with modern hotels
  • Northeast: Historic area with unique stays
  • South Minneapolis: Residential area with comfortable hotels

Top Hotel Categories in Minneapolis

Luxury Hotels

Experience the height of luxury at properties like The Four Seasons Hotel Minneapolis, The W Minneapolis - The Foshay, or The Hewing Hotel. These hotels offer world-class amenities, fine dining, and prime locations near major attractions.

Boutique Hotels

For a more intimate experience, consider boutique hotels in areas like the North Loop or Northeast. These properties often feature unique designs and personalized service.

Budget-Friendly Options

Minneapolis offers several affordable hotel chains and independent properties that provide comfortable stays without breaking the bank. Look for deals in areas like Bloomington or near the airport.

Tips for Booking Hotels in Minneapolis

  • Book at least 2-3 months in advance for peak season
  • Consider major events like the Super Bowl or the Minnesota State Fair
  • Look for hotels with included breakfast
  • Check for hotel packages that include attraction tickets
  • Read recent reviews to ensure the hotel meets your expectations

Best Time to Visit Minneapolis

The best hotel rates are typically available during:

  • January and February (excluding holiday weekends)
  • Late summer (August)
  • Early December (before holiday season)

Transportation Tips

When choosing your hotel, consider:

  • Proximity to major attractions you plan to visit
  • Access to Metro Transit light rail and bus routes
  • Parking availability and costs
  • Distance from Minneapolis-Saint Paul International Airport
