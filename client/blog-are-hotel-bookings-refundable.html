Are Hotel Bookings Refundable?

June 1, 2024 • 3 min read

Whether a hotel booking is refundable depends on the rate and policy you select at the time of reservation. Many hotels offer both refundable and non-refundable rates, so it's important to read the terms carefully before booking.

How to Identify Refundable Rates

  • Look for labels like "Free Cancellation" or "Refundable" during the booking process.
  • Check the cancellation deadlineâ€”most refundable rates allow cancellation up to a certain date.
  • Non-refundable rates are usually cheaper but do not allow changes or refunds.

What to Do If Your Plans Change

  • Contact the hotel or booking site as soon as possible if you need to cancel.
  • Some hotels may offer a partial refund or credit, even for non-refundable bookings, depending on circumstances.
  • Consider travel insurance for added protection against unexpected changes.

Conclusion

Always review the cancellation policy before booking a hotel. Opt for refundable rates if you want flexibility, and keep records of your reservation and any correspondence regarding changes or cancellations.
