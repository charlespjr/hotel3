Payment Options

Secure payment processing with our hotel partners

Payment Processing

AureaVibe does not process payments directly. All payments are handled securely by the hotels or their authorized booking partners when you complete your reservation.

Accepted Payment Methods

Payment options vary by hotel and booking partner, but commonly include:

  • Major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • Debit cards
  • Amazon Pay
  • Klarna (buy now, pay later)
  • Link by Stripe

Currency

Prices are displayed in various currencies. The final payment currency will be determined by the hotel or booking partner's payment system.

Payment Security

All payment transactions are processed using industry-standard encryption and security protocols by our verified partners.

Billing Questions

For payment or billing inquiries, contact the hotel or booking partner directly using the information provided in your booking confirmation.
