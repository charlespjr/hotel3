Hotels Near Portland, OR: Your Complete Guide

June 1, 2024 5 min read

Portland, Oregon's largest city, offers a diverse range of accommodations from luxury hotels in Downtown to boutique properties in trendy neighborhoods. Whether you're visiting for business, exploring the food scene, or enjoying the city's natural beauty, finding the right place to stay in the City of Roses is essential for a great experience. This guide will help you navigate Portland's hotel scene.

Best Areas to Stay in Portland

Portland's neighborhoods each offer unique experiences:

  • Downtown: Luxury hotels and business district
  • Pearl District: Artsy area with boutique hotels
  • Nob Hill: Trendy area with unique stays
  • Eastside: Hip neighborhood with comfortable hotels
  • Lloyd District: Business area with modern accommodations

Top Hotel Categories in Portland

Luxury Hotels

Experience the height of luxury at properties like The Nines, The Hotel deLuxe, or The Heathman Hotel. These hotels offer world-class amenities, fine dining, and prime locations in Portland's most prestigious areas.

Boutique Hotels

For a more intimate experience, consider boutique hotels in areas like the Pearl District or Nob Hill. These properties often feature unique designs and personalized service.

Budget-Friendly Options

Portland offers several affordable hotel chains and independent properties that provide comfortable stays without breaking the bank. Look for deals in areas like the airport or near the convention center.

Tips for Booking Hotels in Portland

  • Book at least 3-4 months in advance for major events
  • Consider food festival schedules when planning your stay
  • Look for hotels with included breakfast (common in Portland)
  • Check for hotel packages that include attraction tickets
  • Read recent reviews to ensure the hotel meets your expectations

Best Time to Visit Portland

The best hotel rates are typically available during:

  • January and February (excluding holiday weekends)
  • Late fall (November)
  • Early spring (March)

Transportation Tips

When choosing your hotel, consider:

  • Proximity to major attractions you plan to visit
  • Access to MAX Light Rail and bus routes
  • Parking availability and costs
  • Distance from Portland International Airport
