How Do Airbnbs Get Cleaned

# How Do Airbnbs Get Cleaned? A Complete Guide to the Cleaning Process When booking an Airbnb, cleanliness is one of the top concerns for guests. A spotless space ensures a comfortable stay and positive reviews. But **how do Airbnbs get cleaned**, and what goes into maintaining high standards? In this guide, we’ll break down the Airbnb cleaning process, best practices for hosts, and tips for guests to ensure a hygienic stay. --- ## **Why Airbnb Cleaning Matters** Cleanliness directly impacts guest satisfaction and reviews. According to Airbnb’s own data, properties with higher cleanliness ratings receive more bookings. Since the pandemic, guests have become even more particular about hygiene, making thorough cleaning essential. ### **Key Benefits of a Clean Airbnb:** - Higher guest ratings and repeat bookings - Compliance with Airbnb’s **Enhanced Cleaning Protocol** (optional but recommended) - Reduced risk of negative reviews or complaints - A safer, healthier environment for guests --- ## **The Standard Airbnb Cleaning Process** Professional Airbnb cleaners follow a systematic approach to ensure every part of the property is spotless. Here’s a step-by-step breakdown: ### **1. Pre-Cleaning Preparation** - **Ventilate the space** – Open windows to let fresh air in. - **Remove trash** – Dispose of all garbage from bins. - **Strip linens** – Remove bed sheets, pillowcases, and towels for washing. ### **2. Dusting and Surface Cleaning** - Wipe down all surfaces (countertops, tables, shelves). - Dust furniture, electronics, and decor. - Clean mirrors and windows for streak-free shine. ### **3. Kitchen Deep Cleaning** - Wash dishes or run the dishwasher. - Sanitize countertops, stove, microwave, and fridge. - Check for expired food and restock essentials (coffee, sugar, etc.). ### **4. Bathroom Sanitization** - Scrub toilet, sink, and shower/tub. - Replace used towels with fresh ones. - Restock toilet paper, soap, and shampoo. ### **5. Floor Care** - Vacuum carpets and rugs. - Mop hard floors with disinfectant. - Check for stains or spills and treat them. ### **6. Final Touches** - Make beds with fresh linens. - Fluff pillows and arrange furniture. - Leave a welcome note or small amenity (e.g., chocolates, water bottles). --- ## **Airbnb’s Enhanced Cleaning Protocol** Since COVID-19, Airbnb introduced an **Enhanced Cleaning Initiative**, a 5-step process that includes: 1. **Preparation** – Using approved disinfectants. 2. **Cleaning** – Removing dust and dirt before sanitizing. 3. **Sanitizing** – Applying high-grade disinfectants to high-touch areas. 4. **Verification** – Cross-checking all cleaned areas. 5. **Reset** – Sealing the space until the next guest arrives. Hosts who follow this protocol receive a **special badge** on their listing, reassuring guests of extra safety measures. --- ## **Who Cleans Airbnbs?** Airbnb hosts have three main options for cleaning: ### **1. Self-Cleaning** - Ideal for hosts who manage their own properties. - Saves money but requires time and effort. ### **2. Professional Cleaning Services** - Many hosts hire **professional cleaners** for efficiency. - Services like **TurnoverBnB** or **Properly** connect hosts with vetted cleaners. ### **3. Co-Host or Property Manager** - A co-host can oversee cleaning between bookings. - Useful for hosts who don’t live near their rental. --- ## **Tips for Airbnb Hosts to Maintain Cleanliness** ✔ **Create a Cleaning Checklist** – Ensure nothing is missed between guests. ✔ **Use High-Quality Supplies** – Invest in disinfectants, microfiber cloths, and a good vacuum. ✔ **Hire Reliable Cleaners** – Read reviews and set clear expectations. ✔ **Automate Laundry** – Use a laundry service for quick turnover. ✔ **Charge a Cleaning Fee** – Offset costs while keeping standards high. --- ## **Tips for Airbnb Guests to Help with Cleaning** Even though cleaning is the host’s responsibility, guests can help by: - Taking out the trash before checkout. - Washing dishes or starting the dishwasher. - Reporting any cleanliness issues immediately. Some hosts offer **self-checkout cleaning options** (e.g., stripping beds) for a smoother turnover. --- ## **Final Thoughts** A clean Airbnb is a **must** for guest satisfaction and successful hosting. Whether you’re a host looking to improve your cleaning routine or a guest curious about the process, understanding **how Airbnbs get cleaned** ensures a better experience for everyone. By following best practices—whether self-cleaning or hiring professionals—hosts can maintain **5-star reviews** and happy guests. ### **Need a Reliable Airbnb Cleaner?** Check out platforms like **TurnoverBnB** or local cleaning services to keep your rental in top shape! --- **Do you have any Airbnb cleaning tips?
