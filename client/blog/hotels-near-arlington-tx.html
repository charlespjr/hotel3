Your Complete Guide to Hotels in Arlington, TX

Arlington, Texas, is a vibrant city located between Dallas and Fort Worth, known for its world-class entertainment, sports venues, and thriving business district. Whether you're visiting for business or pleasure, finding the right hotel is key to enjoying your stay. This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know about hotels in Arlington.

1. Popular Hotel Districts and Neighborhoods

Arlington offers several distinct areas with hotel accommodations, each with its own advantages:

  • Entertainment District - Near AT&T Stadium and Globe Life Field, ideal for sports fans and concert-goers
  • Downtown Arlington - Close to UT Arlington and local dining/nightlife
  • Six Flags/Highway 360 Area - Convenient for families visiting theme parks
  • Interstate 20 Corridor - Budget-friendly options with easy highway access
  • North Arlington - Quieter residential area with some upscale options

2. Average Hotel Prices and Seasonal Variations

Hotel prices in Arlington vary significantly based on season and events:

  • Budget hotels: $60-$100 per night
  • Mid-range hotels: $100-$200 per night
  • Luxury hotels: $200-$400+ per night

Peak seasons (prices 20-50% higher) include:

  • Football season (especially Cowboys home games)
  • Summer (June-August) when Six Flags is busiest
  • Major conventions or events at the convention center

Best value seasons: January-February (excluding Super Bowl if in Dallas) and September-October (excluding football weekends).

3. Types of Hotels Available

Arlington offers accommodations for every taste and budget:

4. Nearby Attractions and Points of Interest

Arlington's hotels place you near some of Texas' top attractions:

  • AT&T Stadium - Home of the Dallas Cowboys
  • Globe Life Field - Texas Rangers baseball stadium
  • Six Flags Over Texas - Major theme park
  • Hurricane Harbor - Water park
  • Arlington Museum of Art
  • International Bowling Museum
  • River Legacy Park - 1,300-acre nature area
  • UT Arlington campus

5. Transportation Options and Accessibility

Getting around Arlington and to nearby cities:

  • Public Transit: Via Arlington (on-demand rideshare service)
  • Ride-sharing: Uber and Lyft widely available
  • Car Rentals: Available at DFW Airport and in the city
  • DFW Airport: 20-30 minutes away by car
  • Dallas Love Field: About 35 minutes away
  • Highway Access: I-20, I-30, and Highway 360 provide easy access

Pro Tip: Many hotels near the entertainment district offer shuttle service to AT&T Stadium and Globe Life Field - ask when booking.

6. Best Times to Visit

The ideal time to visit Arlington depends on your priorities:

  • For sports fans: Football season (September-January) or baseball season (April-October)
  • For families: Spring (March-May) or fall (September-October) for pleasant weather at theme parks
  • For budget travelers: Winter months (excluding holiday periods)
  • For business travelers: Anytime, though convention schedules may affect availability

7. Special Considerations for Travelers

  • Families: Look for hotels with pools and free breakfast; some offer kids-stay-free policies
  • Business travelers: Downtown and convention center area hotels offer best meeting facilities
  • Accessibility: Most major hotels comply with ADA requirements - confirm specific needs when booking
  • Pet owners: Many hotels are pet-friendly, often with weight limits and additional fees
  • International visitors: Major hotels have multilingual staff; DFW Airport offers customs clearance

8. Tips for Finding the Best Deals

Save money on your Arlington hotel stay with these strategies:

  1. Book directly through hotel websites for best rates and perks
  2. Sign up for hotel loyalty programs for member discounts
  3. Check for package deals that include theme park tickets
  4. Be flexible with dates to avoid peak event pricing
  5. Consider staying just outside Arlington in Grand Prairie or Mansfield for lower rates
  6. Look for last-minute deals if your travel dates are flexible
  7. Check Groupon and other discount sites for special offers
