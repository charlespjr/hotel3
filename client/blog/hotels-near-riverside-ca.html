Hotels in Riverside, CA: A Traveler's Guide

Riverside, California, offers a mix of historic charm, cultural attractions, and modern amenities, making it a great destination for business and leisure travelers alike. This guide covers everything you need to know about hotels in Riverside, from pricing and neighborhoods to nearby attractions and travel tips.

1. Popular Hotel Districts and Neighborhoods

Riverside has several key areas where hotels are concentrated:

  • Downtown Riverside – Close to historic sites, restaurants, and entertainment.
  • University District – Near UC Riverside, ideal for visitors to the campus.
  • Magnolia Center – A mix of budget and mid-range hotels with easy freeway access.
  • Riverside Plaza / Tyler Street – Shopping and dining hub with chain hotels.
  • March Air Reserve Base Area – Convenient for military visitors.

2. Average Hotel Prices and Seasonal Variations

Hotel prices in Riverside vary by season and location:

  • Budget hotels: $70–$120 per night
  • Mid-range hotels: $120–$200 per night
  • Luxury hotels: $200+ per night

Peak seasons (higher prices):

  • Spring (March–May) – Pleasant weather and festivals.
  • Fall (September–November) – UC Riverside events and mild temperatures.

Off-peak seasons (lower prices):

  • Summer (June–August) – Hotter weather, fewer tourists.
  • Winter (December–February) – Cooler, but still mild compared to other regions.

3. Types of Hotels Available

Riverside offers a variety of lodging options:

  • Budget Hotels: Motel 6, Super 8, Econo Lodge
  • Mid-Range Hotels: Holiday Inn, Best Western, Hyatt Place
  • Luxury Hotels: Mission Inn Hotel & Spa (iconic historic hotel)
  • Boutique Hotels: The Riverside Hotel, The Salted Pig Inn
  • Extended Stay: Homewood Suites, Residence Inn

4. Nearby Attractions and Points of Interest

Riverside has plenty to see and do:

  • Mission Inn Hotel & Spa – A historic landmark with stunning architecture.
  • Riverside Art Museum – Showcases local and international artists.
  • California Citrus State Historic Park – Celebrates Riverside’s citrus heritage.
  • Mount Rubidoux Park – Scenic hiking trails with city views.
  • Fox Performing Arts Center – Live theater and concerts.

5. Transportation Options and Accessibility

Getting around Riverside is easy with these options:

  • Public Transit: Riverside Transit Agency (RTA) buses serve the city.
  • Rideshare/Taxis: Uber, Lyft, and local taxi services available.
  • Car Rentals: Enterprise, Hertz, and Avis operate in the area.
  • Airport Access: Ontario International Airport (ONT) is 20 miles away.
  • Freeway Access: Close to I-215, CA-91, and CA-60.

6. Best Times to Visit

The ideal times to visit Riverside are:

  • Spring (March–May): Mild weather, blooming gardens.
  • Fall (September–November): Comfortable temperatures, fewer crowds.

Summer can be hot, while winter is cooler but still pleasant.

7. Special Considerations for Travelers

  • Business Travelers: Downtown hotels offer proximity to offices and conference centers.
  • Families: Look for hotels with pools and free breakfast.
  • Pet Owners: Some hotels, like La Quinta, allow pets.
  • Accessibility: Most major hotels comply with ADA standards.

8. Tips for Finding the Best Deals

  • Book in advance for peak seasons.
  • Check hotel websites for exclusive discounts.
  • Use travel apps (Kayak, Expedia, Hotels.com) for price comparisons.
  • Consider weekday stays for lower rates (business hotels drop prices on weekends).
  • Look for package deals that include attractions.

9. Local Amenities and Services

Riverside offers convenient amenities for travelers:

  • Dining: From casual eateries to fine dining (The Salted Pig, Mario’s Place).
  • Shopping: Riverside Plaza, Galleria at Tyler.
  • Grocery Stores: Ralphs, Albertsons, and Trader Joe’s available.
  • Medical Services: Riverside Community Hospital and urgent care centers.

10. Safety and Security Information

Riverside is generally safe, but travelers should:

  • Stay in well-lit areas at night.
  • Use hotel safes for valuables.
  • Lock car doors and avoid leaving belongings visible.
  • Check recent reviews for hotel safety ratings.

For emergencies, dial 911.

Final Thoughts

Whether you're visiting for business, leisure, or a mix of both, Riverside offers a variety of hotels to suit every budget and preference. By planning ahead and considering seasonal trends, you can find the perfect place to stay while enjoying all that this vibrant city has to offer.

Happy travels!
