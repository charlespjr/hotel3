# How Do Airbnb Cancellations Work? A Complete Guide for Guests and Hosts Canceling an Airbnb reservation can be stressful, whether you're a guest with unexpected changes or a host dealing with last-minute adjustments. Understanding Airbnb’s cancellation policies is crucial to avoid penalties, lost income, or negative reviews. In this guide, we’ll break down everything you need to know about Airbnb cancellations, including: - Airbnb’s cancellation policies - How cancellations affect guests and hosts - Steps to cancel a booking - Tips to minimize penalties ## **Understanding Airbnb’s Cancellation Policies** Airbnb offers several cancellation policies that hosts can choose from. These policies determine refund eligibility and penalties. ### **1. Flexible Cancellation Policy** - **Full refund** if canceled at least **24 hours before check-in**. - Partial refund for cancellations made within 24 hours of check-in. ### **2. Moderate Cancellation Policy** - **Full refund** if canceled **5+ days before check-in**. - Partial refund (50%) if canceled within 5 days of check-in. ### **3. Firm Cancellation Policy** - **Full refund** if canceled **30+ days before check-in**. - 50% refund if canceled **14-30 days before check-in**. - **No refund** if canceled within **14 days** of check-in. ### **4. Strict Cancellation Policy** - **50% refund** if canceled **7+ days before check-in**. - **No refund** if canceled within **7 days** of check-in**. ### **5. Long-Term (28+ Nights) Cancellation Policy** - **Full refund** if canceled within **48 hours of booking** (if check-in is at least 28 days away). - After 48 hours, first month is non-refundable. ## **How to Cancel an Airbnb Reservation** ### **For Guests:** 1. **Log in** to your Airbnb account. 2. Go to **"Trips"** and select the reservation. 3. Click **"Cancel Reservation"** and follow the prompts. 4. Review the refund amount based on the host’s policy. 5. Confirm cancellation. ### **For Hosts:** 1. **Go to your Airbnb dashboard**. 2. Select the booking you want to cancel. 3. Click **"Cancel Reservation"** and choose a reason. 4. Confirm cancellation (note: frequent cancellations can penalize hosts). ## **What Happens After a Cancellation?** ### **For Guests:** - Refunds are processed within **10-14 business days**. - Airbnb may issue travel credit in some cases. - Cancellation fees depend on the host’s policy. ### **For Hosts:** - **Cancellation penalties** apply if you cancel too often. - Airbnb may **lower your listing’s ranking** or charge fees. - Blocked dates may be automatically re-opened for new bookings. ## **Tips to Avoid Airbnb Cancellation Issues** ### **For Guests:** ✅ **Book listings with flexible policies** if your plans are uncertain. ✅ **Purchase travel insurance** for unexpected emergencies. ✅ **Communicate with hosts early** if you need to modify or cancel. ### **For Hosts:** ✅ **Choose a cancellation policy** that matches your risk tolerance. ✅ **Offer discounts for rebooking** if a guest cancels last-minute. ✅ **Use Instant Book cautiously** to avoid unwanted reservations. ## **Final Thoughts** Airbnb cancellations can be smooth if you understand the policies and act promptly. Guests should always check cancellation terms before booking, while hosts must balance flexibility with protection against last-minute changes. By following these guidelines, you can minimize stress and financial losses—whether you're traveling or hosting. **Have questions about Airbnb cancellations?
