Hotels Near Oklahoma City, OK: Your Complete Guide

June 1, 2024 5 min read

Oklahoma City, Oklahoma's capital and largest city, offers a diverse range of accommodations from luxury hotels in Bricktown to boutique properties in Midtown. Whether you're visiting for business, sports, or exploring the city's attractions, finding the right place to stay in Oklahoma City is essential for a great experience. This guide will help you navigate Oklahoma City's hotel scene.

Best Areas to Stay in Oklahoma City

Oklahoma City's neighborhoods each offer unique experiences:

  • Bricktown: Entertainment district with luxury hotels
  • Midtown: Artsy area with boutique hotels
  • Downtown: Business district with unique stays
  • Automobile Alley: Historic area with comfortable hotels
  • Plaza District: Cultural area with modern accommodations

Top Hotel Categories in Oklahoma City

Luxury Hotels

Experience the height of luxury at properties like The Skirvin Hilton Oklahoma City, The Ambassador Hotel, or The Colcord Hotel. These hotels offer world-class amenities, fine dining, and prime locations near major attractions.

Boutique Hotels

For a more intimate experience, consider boutique hotels in areas like Midtown or Plaza District. These properties often feature unique designs and personalized service.

Budget-Friendly Options

Oklahoma City offers several affordable hotel chains and independent properties that provide comfortable stays without breaking the bank. Look for deals in areas like Northwest Oklahoma City or near the airport.

Tips for Booking Hotels in Oklahoma City

  • Book at least 2-3 months in advance for peak season
  • Consider major events like NBA games or conventions
  • Look for hotels with included breakfast
  • Check for hotel packages that include attraction tickets
  • Read recent reviews to ensure the hotel meets your expectations

Best Time to Visit Oklahoma City

The best hotel rates are typically available during:

  • January and February (excluding holiday weekends)
  • Late summer (August)
  • Early December (before holiday season)

Transportation Tips

When choosing your hotel, consider:

  • Proximity to major attractions you plan to visit
  • Access to EMBARK bus routes and ride-sharing services
  • Parking availability and costs
  • Distance from Will Rogers World Airport
