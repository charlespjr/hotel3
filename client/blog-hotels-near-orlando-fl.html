Hotels Near Orlando, FL: Your Complete Guide

June 1, 2024 5 min read

Orlando, Florida's theme park capital, offers a diverse range of accommodations from luxury hotels near the parks to boutique properties in Downtown. Whether you're visiting for theme parks, business, or enjoying the city's attractions, finding the right place to stay in Orlando is essential for a great experience. This guide will help you navigate Orlando's hotel scene.

Best Areas to Stay in Orlando

Orlando's neighborhoods each offer unique experiences:

  • International Drive: Tourist area with luxury hotels
  • Downtown: Artsy area with boutique hotels
  • Lake Buena Vista: Disney area with unique stays
  • Universal Boulevard: Theme park area with comfortable hotels
  • Winter Park: Historic area with modern accommodations

Top Hotel Categories in Orlando

Luxury Hotels

Experience the height of luxury at properties like The Ritz-Carlton Orlando, The Four Seasons Resort Orlando, or The Waldorf Astoria Orlando. These hotels offer world-class amenities, fine dining, and prime locations near major attractions.

Boutique Hotels

For a more intimate experience, consider boutique hotels in areas like Downtown or Winter Park. These properties often feature unique designs and personalized service.

Budget-Friendly Options

Orlando offers several affordable hotel chains and independent properties that provide comfortable stays without breaking the bank. Look for deals in areas like International Drive or near the convention center.

Tips for Booking Hotels in Orlando

  • Book at least 3-4 months in advance for peak season
  • Consider theme park schedules when planning your stay
  • Look for hotels with included breakfast (common in Orlando)
  • Check for hotel packages that include park tickets
  • Read recent reviews to ensure the hotel meets your expectations

Best Time to Visit Orlando

The best hotel rates are typically available during:

  • January and February (excluding holiday weekends)
  • Late summer (August)
  • Early December (before holiday season)

Transportation Tips

When choosing your hotel, consider:

  • Proximity to major attractions you plan to visit
  • Access to Lynx bus routes and ride-sharing services
  • Parking availability and costs
  • Distance from Orlando International Airport
