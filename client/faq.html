Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about AureaVibe

What is AureaVibe?

AureaVibe is a hotel rate comparison platform that helps you find the best available prices across multiple booking sites. We don't own or operate hotels - we simply help you discover the best rates available.

How does AureaVibe work?

We search and compare hotel rates from various booking platforms and hotel websites to show you the best available prices. When you find a rate you like, we direct you to complete your booking with the hotel or booking partner.

Do you charge any fees?

No, AureaVibe is completely free to use. We earn a small commission from our hotel partners when you complete a booking, but this never affects the price you pay.

Who handles my booking and payment?

Your booking and payment are processed directly by the hotel or their authorized booking partner. AureaVibe facilitates the rate comparison but is not involved in the actual booking transaction.

What about cancellations and changes?

All cancellation and modification policies are set by the individual hotels, not by AureaVibe. Please review the specific cancellation terms for your booking, as they vary by property and rate type.

How accurate are the rates shown?

We update rates frequently throughout the day, but prices can change rapidly. The final rate will be confirmed when you complete your booking with the hotel or booking partner.

Can I book directly through AureaVibe?

AureaVibe is a rate comparison service. When you find your preferred rate, we'll direct you to the hotel or booking partner's website to complete your reservation securely.

What if I need help with my booking?

For booking-related issues, contact the hotel or booking partner directly. For questions about using AureaVibe's search and comparison features, our support team is here to help.
